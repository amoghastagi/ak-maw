Welcome to AK Make-A-Wish!

At AK Make-A-Wish, we are committed to raising awareness and funds to support the Make-A-Wish Foundation in granting life-changing wishes for children facing critical illnesses. Partnering with Make-A-Wish Central & Western North Carolina (CWNC), we are proud to be their largest student-led organization. Our mission is to bring hope, strength, and joy to these children by organizing school-wide fundraisers, events, and activities that encourage community involvement and support. As a member, you'll have the chance to develop leadership skills, collaborate with a passionate team of peers, and make a real impact on the lives of families in need. Join us today and help turn dreams into reality!
